Basic Pressing W,A,D keys will move the player and pressing SPACE will attack
Crafting Press C to open the crafting menu and click on the icons to see the recipe
Zombies Zombies will spawn periodically in different group sizes
Building Blocks can be broken and placed anywhere. Planks can only be moved through by players.
Boss Once 50 zombies have been killed. The boss will spawn and begin attacking the player.
